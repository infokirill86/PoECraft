# Participant and Architecture Critique

## Direction

M47 is the right next project direction because Jawbone and Reveal are directly useful to the intended physical-quarterstaff route and the state/pool foundations already exist. It is not infrastructure drift.

## Boundary correction

The requested surface is not one safe runtime batch.

- Base Jawbone installation and base Reveal are one coherent state-transition family.
- Necromancy side Omens, named-Lich guarantees, Abyssal Echoes, and Omen of Light are modifier layers with separate admission and sampling questions.
- Fracture interaction is PD-013 and cannot be bundled into base admission while sources conflict.

The prepared YAML is unusually detailed, but detail is not evidence. Its side-first Jawbone lottery, replacement rules, sequential Reveal sampling, exclusive compatibility, Lich guarantee implementation, and reroll independence must remain candidate policy until explicitly gated.

## Recommended split

- `M47-A`: three base Jawbones plus base Reveal, only after the required project-model decisions.
- `M47-B`: Desecration modifier/Omen layer.
- `M47-C`: Fracture interaction and PD-013.

This split preserves momentum while preventing one uncertain probability model from contaminating every later route calculation.
