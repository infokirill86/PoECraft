# PoE1 Analogy Notes

PoE1 analogy is support only, not authority for PoE2.

## Chaos Orb

PoE1 Chaos Orb behavior is not a useful direct analogue for PoE2 base Chaos, because PoE1 Chaos rerolls a rare item rather than performing PoE2's remove-then-add behavior.

Use:

- historical contrast only;
- not a source for M37 implementation.

## Orb of Annulment

PoE1 Annulment wording and wiki mechanics also use random modifier removal language.

Use:

- weak support that "random modifier removal" is normally modeled as random over eligible modifiers;
- not proof of PoE2 server implementation.

## Exalted Orb

PoE1 Exalted and PoE2 Exalted both use add-a-random-modifier language.

Use:

- weak support for a generated modifier pool model;
- not proof of prefix/suffix selection ordering.

## Bottom line

PoE1 analogues do not decide M37. The load-bearing PoE2 decision comes from PoE2DB / PoE2 Wiki wording plus repo source policy.
