# Plain-language summary for Kirill

Perfect Essence is the logical next capability: it works on a Rare item, removes one modifier, and installs one exact modifier chosen by the Essence. That directly extends the accepted Greater Essence work and can remain separate from Omens.

The simple wording hides two important questions. If the item has no free prefix slot but the Essence adds a prefix, the game appears to choose a removable prefix rather than risk removing a suffix and then failing. Public official wording does not state that probability rule precisely. Also, current 0.5 patch wording limits an item to one crafted modifier. That strongly suggests a Greater-Essence item cannot simply receive a Perfect Essence, but the official text does not say whether Perfect Essence may remove and replace the existing crafted modifier.

Therefore the six-row family is a good next wave, but it is not safe to implement until ChatGPT/User explicitly selects the project-model removal and repeat-application rules after Claude review.
