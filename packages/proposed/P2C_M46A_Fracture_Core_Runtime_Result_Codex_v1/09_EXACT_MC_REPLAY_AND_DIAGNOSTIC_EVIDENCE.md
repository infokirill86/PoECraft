# Exact, MC, Replay, and Diagnostic Evidence

## Exact/oracle

Exact paths are enumerated as canonical rational branches over installed instance identities. Exact terminal aggregation is by canonical terminal-state hash, and total exact mass conservation is a hard assertion.

## Seeded Monte Carlo

The executor uses the accepted deterministic decision source and records candidate digest, selected candidate identity, duplicate ordinal, pre/post state hashes, and pool fingerprint. Fixed-seed replay produces the same result hash and decision records.

## Negative control

A deliberately non-uniform injected candidate pool is rejected by the load-bearing uniform-unit-weight invariant. This proves the suite can detect the central Fracture selection defect.

## Public-output boundary

No probability values are published here. Internal exact/MC evidence remains project-model validation only.
