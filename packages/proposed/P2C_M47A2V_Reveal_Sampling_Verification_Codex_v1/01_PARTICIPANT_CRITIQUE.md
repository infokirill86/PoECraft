# Participant / Architecture Critique

## Conclusion

M47-A2V is the right next boundary. Reveal runtime should not be implemented while D4 - the probability-bearing offer sampler - is unknown. An offline evidence layer is cheaper and safer than choosing a convenient algorithm and later rebuilding exact/MC truth around it.

## Material correction to the implied capture boundary

Three visible offers alone cannot distinguish tier-row weighted sampling from family-first or other mixture models. D4 analysis also needs:

- repeated observations from the same reproducible setup;
- exact installed families/groups and placeholder side;
- an independently reconstructed eligible pool snapshot;
- generation weights when available.

When those inputs are absent, the tool must say `inconclusive`; it must not infer an algorithm from frequencies alone.

## Why the boundary is right-sized

- It includes the schema, capture protocol, analysis, and tests needed to make evidence usable.
- It does not include Reveal execution, Echoes rerolling, offer selection, or accepted probability policy.
- It reuses accepted data identifiers and constraints without creating a second mechanics database.
- It keeps the final project-model decision with ChatGPT/User after Claude audit.

No material objection remains after applying the stricter D4 evidence requirement.
