# Probability Impact and Stop Rules

## Why D3-D5 matter

- D3 changes the probability that an exclusive modifier appears at all.
- D4 changes every offer and offer-set probability, including family/tier outcomes and duplicate-path aggregation.
- D5 changes whether some states have a valid transition and whether probability mass is preserved as no-transition.
- Echoes constraint persistence changes the entire reroll pool and cannot be treated as a cosmetic second draw.

## Mandatory hard stops for implementation

- D3-D5 are not explicitly accepted by ChatGPT/User.
- A canonical modifier row cannot be resolved.
- Exact pool/category/family/group digest cannot be reproduced.
- Exact path mass fails conservation.
- The item changes between offer generation and selection.
- The implementation tries to rank or automatically choose offers.
- Echoes, named-Lich, Necromancy, Light, or Putrefaction enters base Reveal scope.
- Public numeric probability output or PD-013 closure is requested without a separate gate.

## No public numeric release

Evidence sample sizes may be recorded internally for provenance. Offer probabilities, rates, expected attempts, rankings, and crafting advice remain quarantined.
