# Boundaries and Risks

## Proposed, not accepted

M47-A1 runtime remains proposed until Claude audit and a later ChatGPT/User gate. The three rows are runtime-admitted only inside this proposed implementation delta; this package does not self-accept them.

## Explicitly closed

- Reveal runtime and all D3-D5 offer/sampling decisions;
- Echoes runtime;
- Omen of Light, Necromancy/named-Lich Omens, and Putrefaction;
- more than one hidden-or-revealed Desecrated object;
- revealed-Desecrated plus Fracture runtime;
- PD-013 closure;
- planner, optimizer, economics, advice, ranking, and public numeric release.

## Audit watchpoints

- Confirm D1-A never offers replacement while either side has capacity.
- Confirm D2-A is combined-instance uniform, not side-first.
- Confirm duplicate installed identities remain distinct replacement paths.
- Confirm Gnawed item-level maximum and Ancient Reveal MML are row-driven.
- Confirm failure never exposes a remove-only state.
- Confirm the hidden placeholder counts toward the Fracture minimum but never enters the target pool.
- Confirm revealed Desecrated modifier instances remain rejected by Fracture.
- Confirm the semantic fingerprint delta is limited to the documented admission/policy surface.
